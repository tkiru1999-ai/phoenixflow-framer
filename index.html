About

Built by someone who has done the busywork.

PhoenixFlow UK started after years in SaaS and loan sales — jobs where I lived inside inboxes, spreadsheets, and CRMs, and watched good leads die because nobody had time to follow up. The pattern was always the same: the work wasn't hard, it was just relentless. So I started automating it — first for myself, then for the small businesses around me.

Now that's all I do: build automation workflows that give owners their hours back. No jargon, no six-month projects. One useful flow at a time.
